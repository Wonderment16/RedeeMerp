# RedeeMERP 90-Second Judge Script

## 0-15 seconds

"RedeeMERP helps people find their way around RCCG Camp without staring at their phone. The idea is simple: speak where you want to go, put your phone away, and listen."

## 15-35 seconds

Open the app in mobile Chrome. Point out the RCCG-colored map, destination pins, search bar, mic button, and popular places.

Say: "I can search manually, but the product is voice-first."

## 35-55 seconds

Tap the mic and say one of:

- "I want food"
- "Take me to the big church"
- "Where do buses stop?"
- "Youth"

Show the destination being selected.

## 55-75 seconds

Tap **Start Navigation**. The app speaks human instructions only:

- "Navigation started. Keep moving straight."
- "Turn right now."
- "You're on the right path."

Mention that the app avoids distance-heavy instructions because visitors need simple guidance.

## 75-90 seconds

Tap the RCCG logo three times to activate demo mode if live GPS is not practical. It simulates walking from Main Gate to Youth Centre and fires the voice flow automatically.

Close with: "For the hackathon demo, this proves the navigation experience even indoors. Outdoors, it uses live GPS and Google walking directions."
